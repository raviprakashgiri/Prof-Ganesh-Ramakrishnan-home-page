Research Projects

  • Application Classification and Recommendation Engine App in Android

    • The purpose is to come up with a thorough, extensive and accurate algorithm that makes use of extensive pattern matching and machine learning algorithms to recommend relevant apps to users, based on feature vectors and certain other parameters. This algorithm is very much similar to Google's PageRank System. It also involves studying user behaviour by tracking his details from various apps like Google play store, running predictive strategies and suggesting relevant recommendations on regular timings.
    (Research Topics : Machine Learning, Artificial Intelligence, Probability and Statistics, Information Retrieval, Ontology)
    Requirements: Applicants are expected to be proficient in JAVA and have a basic idea of how Android Applications work. Additionally, they should have a high level research background and be motivated enough to work in the above research categories.     Know More...

  • Development and Demonstration of mobile based ICT tool for Farmers (Rural IVRS)

    • Goal is to generate employment in village areas by helping the farmers with agricultural facitlities by broadcasting/ recieving the message by leveraging the power of spoken voice,mobile phones and message.
    Technologies used: JAVA, JSP, AJAX, JQUERY, HTML are used as front ends and Servlet as a back end. Know More...

  • Development of an Android based app for facilitating R&D and learning in rural areas.

    • Description: The purpose is :-
    1. Educating the villagers with information and knowledge
    2. Empowering them to solve their own problems proactively, with use of what is accessible to them.
    For this we have to create a knowledge management system which will preserve the knowledge of traditional practices by using new technology in scientific way.
